ï»¿Z-0253.1HOUSE BILL 1144State of Washington69th Legislature2025 Regular SessionByRepresentatives Lekanoff, Low, Ramel, Shavers, and Paul; by request of Administrative Office of the CourtsPrefiled 12/30/24.Read first time 01/13/25.Referred to Committee on Civil Rights & Judiciary.AN ACT Relating to adding an additional superior court judge in Skagit county; and amending RCW 2.08.063.BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WASHINGTON:Sec. 1. RCW 2.08.063 and 2024 c 112 s 1 are each amended to read as follows:There shall be in the county of Lincoln one judge of the superior court; in the county of Skagit, ((four))five judges of the superior court; in the county of Walla Walla, two judges of the superior court; in the county of Whitman, one judge of the superior court; in the county of Yakima, eight judges of the superior court; in the county of Adams, one judge of the superior court; in the county of Whatcom, five judges of the superior court.--- END ---
