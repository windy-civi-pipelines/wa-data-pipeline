RCW 43.216.556 and 2021 c 199 s 208 are each amended to read as follows:(1) Funding for the program of early learning established under this chapter must be appropriated to the department. Entitlement under this section is voluntary enrollment.(4) School districts and approved community-based early learning providers may contract with the department to provide services under the program.
